About Vero Beach Concrete Concepts

Founded in 2003 by master craftsman Tom Miller, Vero Beach Concrete Concepts began with a simple mission: to provide the highest quality concrete solutions with unmatched customer service. What started as a small family business has grown into Vero Beach's most trusted concrete contractor, serving over 5,000 satisfied customers throughout Indian River County.

Our core values of integrity, craftsmanship, and innovation drive everything we do. We believe in using only premium materials, employing skilled local craftsmen, and staying ahead of industry trends to deliver concrete solutions that stand the test of time. Every project we undertake is backed by our commitment to excellence and our comprehensive warranty program.

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve in Vero Beach?

We provide comprehensive concrete services throughout Indian River County, including Vero Beach, Sebastian, Fellsmere, and surrounding areas within a 30-mile radius of our headquarters.

How long does a typical concrete project take?

Residential projects typically take 3-5 days for preparation and installation, plus 24-48 hours for initial curing. Larger commercial projects may require 1-2 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

What type of warranty do you offer?

Our work comes with a comprehensive 10-year warranty covering structural integrity and workmanship. We also provide a 2-year warranty on decorative finishes and sealants.

What payment options do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, checks, and offer flexible payment plans through our financing partners. Commercial projects can be arranged with progress payments.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes! We offer free, detailed estimates within 24 hours of your consultation. Our quotes include all materials, labor, and permit costs with no hidden fees.
